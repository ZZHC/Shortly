What's new in beta 2

This build contains:

  • New feature: a keyboard hotkey for shortening.
    (Default off; default hotkey: option/alt + S)
  • New feature: a quick option menu. Hold on the shorten button on Safari's toolbar to display. (Safari 5.1+ only)
  • Improved Italiano locale thanks to Raffaele Viggiani
  • Improved Simplified Chinese locale thanks to @catjia1990
  • Updating jsOAuth library to ver 1.3
  • Localable strings for new features.
  • Other bug fixes and enhancements

Or you can visit Gihub for commit log of v2.0 beta 2.
